Dataset in "data2" folder

Accidents_year.csv: accident data on M, A(M) and A roads in the UK from the year of 2009 to 2014. Extracted from original dataset in "data" folder.

Casualties_year.csv: Casualty data on M, A(M) and A roads in the UK from the year of 2009 to 2014. Extracted from original casualty dataset in "data" folder. 

Casualties_M_year.csv: Casualty data on M, A(M) roads in the UK from the year of 2009 to 2014. Extracted from Casualties_year.csv.
